Features

Everything You Need, Powered by AI

Six core features, each enhanced by MiMo's reasoning models to deliver insights no traditional dashboard can.

🔗

Multi-Chain Portfolio

Unified view across 8+ chains — Ethereum, Base, Arbitrum, Polygon, BSC, Optimism, Avalanche, and Solana. Track every position in one place.

🤖

AI Yield Optimization

MiMo analyzes yield patterns across 50+ protocols, predicts APY changes, and recommends optimal rebalancing strategies.

💬

Natural Language Queries

Ask questions in plain English. "What's the safest 10%+ APY on Arbitrum?" — MiMo understands context and delivers actionable answers.

⚠️

Smart Contract Risk

AI-driven risk scoring analyzes audits, transaction patterns, TVL trends, and contract complexity to rate every protocol.

🌉

Cross-Chain Bridges

Find the cheapest, fastest, or safest route for any cross-chain transfer. MiMo compares bridge fees, slippage, and security.

⛽

Gas Optimization

MiMo tracks gas patterns across chains and recommends optimal transaction timing to minimize costs.
